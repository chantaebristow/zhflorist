About

We’re Z&H Florist, a local flower shop based in Vancouver. We love helping people brighten their day with fresh, beautiful flowers. Whether you’re celebrating something big, saying thank you, or just want a little color in your home, we’re here to create something special for you. Every arrangement is made with care, and we’re always happy to chat and help you find the perfect blooms. Jenny puts love and care into every bouquet, and our team is always happy to help you pick out something that feels just right.
